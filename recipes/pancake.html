Pancake

Description

easy, fast and minimal recipe.

  • 4 eggs
  • 8 large spoons of white flour
  • half a cup of milk
    Steps
  1. Puts all the eggs in a bowl.
  2. put 8 large spoons of white flour in too.
  3. pour half a cup of milk in.
  4. mix well.
  5. oil a frying pan.
  6. cook until golden brown.
  7. eat and enjoy.
